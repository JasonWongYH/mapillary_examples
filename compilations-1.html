--- layout: compilations title: Mapillary Around the World smalltitle: Selected contributions from the Golden Gate and beyond. description:

Start at the Golden Gate bridge and end up somewhere surprising!

These are parts from different Mapillary sequences, dynamically pulled together into one route of about 2500 images length. They demonstrate that you can build your own navigation anywhere on the planet.

Look at this pages source and the code on Github and get going yourself!
