--- layout: routes title: Commute Limhamn - Lund smalltitle: A multi-legged commute trip description:

This example demonstrates a trip consisting of different legs containing approx 2500 images from 15 parts of different Mapillary sequences, with different modes of transportation.

These legs can be mapped programmatically to a routing planner in order to provide visual guidance and travel help for transportation applications.

  • Bus 34 Limhamn->Malmö Värnhem
  • Walk from Bus 34->Bus 3 at Malmö Värnhem
  • Bus 3 Malmö Värnhem->Malmö Central
  • Walk from Bus 3 Malmö Central->Platform 3a Malmö Central
  • Train Malmö Central->Lund Central

See the full view of the area on Mapillary for the original sequences material.
